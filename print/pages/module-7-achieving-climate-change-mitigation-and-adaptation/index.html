Module 7: Achieving climate change mitigation and adaptation

The aim of this module was to create a clear understanding of the principles of sustainable and climate adapted design and retrofitting, as it applies to small scale development. The module provided knowledge and development management skills to support the role of officers to achieve climate change mitigation and adaptation by successful implementing climate change and renewable energy policies.
