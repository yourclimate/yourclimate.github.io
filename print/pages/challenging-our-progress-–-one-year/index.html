Challenging our Progress – One Year On

The Climate Change Partnership for Yorkshire & Humber held its annual conference on 23 April 2010 'Challenging our Progress – One Year On'.

Following key note speakers, from the Committee on Climate Change and the consultancy Acclimatise, delegates were invited review to the Partnership’s work programme and identify new areas of work that needed to be prioritised.
