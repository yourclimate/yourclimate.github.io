Follow Your Climate on Twitter

DateWednesday, December 21, 2011
Topic Governance [4] Historic Environment [5] Transport [6] Biodiversity & Natural Environment [7] Civic Participation [8] Housing [9] Planning, Land & Flood Defence [10] Pollution [11] Greenhouse Gas Emissions & Climate Change [12] Energy, Waste & Natural Resources [13] Sustainable Development [14] Environment [15] Governance & Civic Participation [16] Infrastructure [17] Quality of Life [18]

Your Climate has embraced the new and is now an active Twitter user and participant. We have been running our account for a couple of months to test the water and gather feedback as to whether it is a useful part of how we communicate our plans, actions and activities.

We are pretty taken with what we have seen so far and so we will be using our account @your_climate [2] to communicate a whole raft of new activities for 2012 including updates regarding the Climate Change Skills Fund and the implications of the upcoming UK Climate Change Risk Assessment for Yorkshire and Humber.

So follow us and stay completely up to date and in the loop.

Plus if there any any feeds that we should be following then drop us a line at newsletter@yourclimate.org [3] and we will add you to the mix.

Finally, as it is the festive season, a very Merry Christmas to all our chums Yorkshire, Humber and beyond!
