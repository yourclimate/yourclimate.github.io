Eco-Schools is an international award scheme which schools can use to make sure that they are working hard to be more and more eco-friendï»¿ly. Sheffield City Council are promoting Eco-Schools to support our schools to take action to help improve the local environment and protect Planet Earth.

Schools are at the heart of every one of our communities. Our schools take steps to improve their environmental performance through the curriculum, on the campus and in their community.
