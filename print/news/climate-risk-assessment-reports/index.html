Reporting Authority Climate Risk Assessment Reports

DateMonday, January 31, 2011
Topic Governance [12] Planning, Land & Flood Defence [13] Greenhouse Gas Emissions & Climate Change [14] Energy, Waste & Natural Resources [15] Sustainable Development [16] Environment [17] Governance & Civic Participation [18] Infrastructure [19]

The first seven reports submitted by early reporting authorities are available below. A brief government document summarising the initial findings from these reports [2] (PDF 400 KB) is also available.

  1. National Grid Gas Plc [3] (PDF 2.4 MB)
  2. National Grid Electricity Transmission [4] (PDF 2.3 MB)
  3. Environment Agency [5] (PDF 1 MB)
  4. Trinity House [6] (PDF 1.2 MB)
  5. Highways Agency [7] (PDF 1 MB) (interim)
  6. Network Rail [8] (PDF 375 KB) (interim)
  7. Natural England [9] (PDF 2.5 MB) (interim)

Over the course of 2011 DEFRA will receive and publish, after approval by Defra’s Secretary of State, the remainder of the reports, covering the sectors listed below. In January 2012 they will publish summaries for each sector and an overall review of the first round of reporting.

  • Water utilities
  • Electricity generators
  • Electricity transmitters
  • Electricity distribution
  • Gas transporters
  • Rail
  • Strategic airports
  • Harbour authorities
  • Economic regulators

If you have any questions or require further information on the Adaptation Reporting Power, please contact: acc_reportingpower@defra.gsi.gov.uk [10].

Or for more information please visit http://ww2.defra.gov.uk/environment/climate/sectors/reporting-authorities/ [11]
