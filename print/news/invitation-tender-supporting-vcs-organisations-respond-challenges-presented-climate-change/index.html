Invitation to tender: supporting VCS organisations to respond to the challenges presented by climate change

DateFriday, August 6, 2010
Topic Greenhouse Gas Emissions & Climate Change [4] Environment [5] Quality of Life [6]

North Yorkshire County Council (for partners in the York and N. Yorks sub-region) has been awarded funding to support VCS organisations to respond to the challenges presented by climate change. We are inviting suitably qualified and experienced organisations to submit tenders to undertake this project.

  • Download tender document [2] (pdf)

TENDER CLOSING DATE/TIME: Monday 20th September 2010, 9am

If you have any quesions please contact Jonathan Spencer - Jonathan.Spencer@northyorks.gov.uk [3] - (01609) 533488.
