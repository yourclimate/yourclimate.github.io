Green design competition from CO2Sense

DateMonday, November 14, 2011
Topic Economy [5] Productivity & Economic Growth [6] Innovation [7] Greenhouse Gas Emissions & Climate Change [8] Energy, Waste & Natural Resources [9] Investment [10] Sustainable Development [11] Environment [12]

You could save thousands of pounds by reviewing your product design. Want £10,000 from CO2Sense to help you to do this?

If your new product has an environmental benefit – for example, it could be recyclable or made from recycled materials, or it maybe it takes less energy to make or use – then you could win up to £10,000 worth of consultancy to help develop it, cut its carbon emissions, and bring it closer to market.

But you’ll need to hurry – the deadline for entries is 5pm on Friday December 2nd.

CO2Sense are looking for the best, most innovative new designs, which can win up to £10,000 worth of consultancy each. The winner can use their consultancy on anything from design support to prototype construction, materials, packaging, branding, and assistance with business cases and financial projections. There's even the possibility of getting investment from CO2Sense or our partners.

Any small or medium-sized business in Yorkshire and the Humber can apply. You can enter any type of product, as long as it has some sort of environmental benefit. We’ve previously worked with products from street-lighting and building products to solar panels and spice-racks.

Applying is simple – just download the application form here [2] (guidance notes available here [3]) and send it back to us by the end of Friday 2nd December.

For more information please visit www.co2sense.co.uk/competition [4]
