Energy Security and Green Economy Bill

DateThursday, December 9, 2010
Topic Economy [4] Governance [5] Greenhouse Gas Emissions & Climate Change [6] Energy, Waste & Natural Resources [7] Sustainable Development [8] Environment [9] Infrastructure [10]

The Government has published its new Bill which they claim will drive the provision of energy efficiency for homes and businesses, enable low carbon energy supply and create a fair energy market. As such it is a key piece of legislation that will be crucial if we are to achieve the UK’s contribution to carbon emission reductions over the next decade and beyond.

  • More on the Government's Bill [2]

Friends of the Earth has produced a short guide [3] to what should be included in the final legislation when it is passed in 2011. Key demands include:

  • Local carbon budgets for all local authorities;
  • A national retrofit scheme for UK homes
  • Emissions Performance Standards for coal and gas power stations;
  • Extension of Feed In Tariffs to cover installations up to 10MW.
