Request from Sheffield Renewables

DateMonday, June 13, 2011
Topic Biodiversity & Natural Environment [5] Civic Participation [6] Innovation [7] Enterprise [8] Greenhouse Gas Emissions & Climate Change [9] Utilities & Broadband [10] Energy, Waste & Natural Resources [11] Investment [12] Employment [13] Environment [14] Quality of Life [15]

The Sheffield Renewables [2] Jordan Dam project is moving forward rapidly, and Sheffield Renewables are now looking for grants to help build the scheme. The online community EnergyShare is offering up to £100,000 for projects like Jordan Dam. To win these grants, Sheffield Renewables need to get as many ‘supporters’ as possible signed up on their website by the end of the month. If you want to help by signing up, just follow the instructions below.

Why you might want to do this:

  • It could win us much of the grant funding which Jordan Dam needs to go ahead
  • It’s a great way to tell us you’re out there, and willing to help
  • It will publicly show the level of support we have

How to become a Sheffield Renewables ‘supporter’ on EnergyShare:

  1. Go to our EnergyShare profile page here: http://www.energyshare.com/sheffield-renewables/ [3] and click “Support this group” on the right hand side of the page.
  2. Click the “Sign Up” button, choose a user name, enter your email address and fill out the other questions with the red asterisks beside them. When this is complete, click “Sign Up” and the site will send you an email.
  3. Open this email, and click the link in to automatically complete your registration and register your support for us.

We think this is a great way to show the level of support we have - please take a few moments to support Sheffield Renewables.

For more information contact Jean Tinsley - jean.tinsley@phonecoop.coop [4]
