Presentations available from ‘New Beginnings and New Challenges in the Sub-Regions (Sheffield City Region)'

DateWednesday, November 10, 2010
Topic Economy [9] Governance [10] Civic Participation [11] Sectors Clusters & Economic Structure [12] Planning, Land & Flood Defence [13] Sustainable Development [14] Environment [15] Governance & Civic Participation [16] Infrastructure [17]

Thank you to everyone that attended the YHREF workshop event 'New Beginnings and New Challenges in the Sub-Regions (Sheffield City Region)' yesterday and thanks to all our excellent speakers.

The presentations from the day are now available to download as sensibly sized pdfs from the YHREF website. They can be found on the dedicated Sheffield City Region page - www.yhref.org.uk/pages/sheffield-city-region [2] - or by clicking on the links below.

  • Introduction: New beginnings (Presentation) - Simon Bowens, YHREF Chair & Friends of the Earth (Nov 2010) [3]
  • Sheffield City Region (Presentation) - Jenny Poxon, Head of Spatial Planning, Sheffield City Region Executive Team (Nov 2010) [4]
  • Environment Funding Forum (Presentation) - Barbara Singh, Natural England (Nov 2010) [5]
  • Developing the Environmental Evidence Base (Presentation) - Amanda Crossfield, Yorkshire Futures (Nov 2010) [6]

If you would like more information or would like to get involved either with the next workshop (Hull and Humber Ports [7]) or the Regional Conference (early 2011) then please email event@yhref.org.uk [8] with your thoughts and wisdom.
