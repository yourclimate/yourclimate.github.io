Presentations available from ‘New Beginnings and New Challenges in the Sub-Regions (Humber Sub Region)'

DateTuesday, January 18, 2011
Topic Economy [8] Investment [9] Employment [10] Sustainable Development [11] Environment [12] Governance & Civic Participation [13] Infrastructure [14] Quality of Life [15]

Thank you to everyone who attended our 4th workshop event focused on the fate of the environment in a new coalition shaped world. A full report from the day will follow shortly but in the meantime here are the presentations for you to download and browse at your leisure.

All are pdf downloads and all should be small enough to prevent any slower computers from grumbling.

  • "Introduction and scene setting [2]" -Simon Bowens, FOE and YHREF Chair
  • "Environment Funding Focus Group [3]" - Barbara Singh, Natural England
  • "Developing the Environmental Evidence Base [4]" - Amanda Crossfield, Yorkshire Futures
  • "Humber Industry Nature Conservation Association [5]" Darren Clarke, Conservation Manager
  • "Natural England’s work on the Humber Estuary [6]" - Will MacLennan, Natural England

For more information on our workshop series please visit the sub regional section of the website which can be found at www.yhref.org.uk/pages/yhref-and-sub-regions [7]
