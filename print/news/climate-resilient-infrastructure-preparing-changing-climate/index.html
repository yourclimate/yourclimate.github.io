Climate Resilient Infrastructure: Preparing for a Changing Climate

DateMonday, May 9, 2011
Topic Economy [4] Governance [5] Productivity & Economic Growth [6] Transport [7] Biodiversity & Natural Environment [8] Housing [9] Innovation [10] Planning, Land & Flood Defence [11] Pollution [12] Public Services Performance [13] Enterprise [14] Greenhouse Gas Emissions & Climate Change [15] Investment [16] Sustainable Development [17] Environment [18] Governance & Civic Participation [19] Infrastructure [20]

On 9th May 2011, Defra’s Secretary of State launched Climate Resilient Infrastructure, a cross-Government report which outlines the challenges to the transport, energy, water and ICT infrastructure sectors to adapting to climate change. 

  • Download the complete report [2] (pdf)

This document has been produced as a response to calls from industry – infrastructure owners, investors and insurers – for a Government vision and policy on adapting infrastructure to climate change. It is designed to catalyse action to adapt infrastructure in the energy, ICT, transport and water sectors (infrastructure networks).

For more information please visit www.defra.gov.uk/publications/2011/05/09/climate-resilient-infrastructure/  [3]
