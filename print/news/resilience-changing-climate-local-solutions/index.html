Resilience to a Changing Climate: Local Solutions

DateMonday, June 30, 2014
Topic Environment [2]

As part of the Climate UK family we celebrated progress over the last two and a half years with a special sold out event for local and national stakeholders on Monday 16th June in the London Living Room at City Hall. The event 'Resilience to a changing climate: local solutions' featured 11 round table discussions showcasing the work of Climate UK's local partnerships, speakers including Daniel Johns from the Adaptation Sub Committee, Katharine Knox from the Joseph Rowntree Foundation, Mark Wray from the Technology Strategy Board and a panel discussion chaired by Rupert Clubb, Chair of Defra's Local Authority Adaptation Panel.

At the event Climate UK published its Annual Report 2014 and this together with the keynote presentations from the day are available to download from the Climate UK website at http://climateuk.net/news/climate-uk-celebrates-londons-living-room [please copy this link into your web browser].
