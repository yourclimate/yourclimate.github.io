Get your hands on a Yorkshire Futures publication

DateTuesday, May 24, 2011
Topic Economy [4] Productivity & Economic Growth [5] Education & Skilled People [6] Environment [7] Governance & Civic Participation [8] Infrastructure [9] Population, Settlements & Migration [10] Quality of Life [11]

Yorkshire Futures have printed copies of research publications available to collect from our offices in central Leeds. The publications cover a wide range of social, economic and environmental topics relating to Yorkshire and Humber and include:

  • What Works in the Arts - Good Practice in Yorkshire & Humber
  • Age Matters - realising the potential of an ageing population
  • Quality of Life in Yorkshire & Humber: What matters?
  • Economic Impact Assessment of Faith Communities in Yorkshire & Humber
  • Teacher Recruitment and Retention in Yorkshire & Humber
  • Weathering the Storm - Yorkshire & Humber Regional Adaptation Study
  • Progress in the Region 2009 (March 2010)

A full list of the publications available to collect from Yorkshire Futures is available to download here [2].

If you would like to obtain any of the publications, multiple copies are available, please phone Yorkshire Futures on 0113 394 9988 or e-mail: info@yorkshirefutures.com [3]  

The deadline for expressions of interest is Friday 10 June 2011.
