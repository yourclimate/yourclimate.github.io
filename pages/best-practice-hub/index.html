Best Practice Hub

Best Practice Hub

The Climate Change Skills Fund (CCSF) programme aims to build capacity and improve the skills of those involved in dealing with climate change issues in the planning process. Sheffield City Council has led on the delivery of the Best Practice Hub, which provides an online hub of climate change best practice case studies from across the region.

These case studies cover a range of topics relating to mitigating climate change and acting to adapt to the consequences of a changing climate.  The case studies can be downloaded below.

Have you got a case study you would like to share?

If you have an example of best practice and would like to include this on the Best Practice Hub, please download and complete the template below and email it to the Project Team via the contact information below.
