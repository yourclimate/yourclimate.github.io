CCSF Module 1

Module 1: LDF/plan making evidence base and Implementation of the Yorkshire and Humber renewable and Low Carbon Energy Study, 2011

This module provided an understanding of how the findings of the Yorkshire and Humber Renewable and Low Carbon Study 2011 can be translated and how the study can be used as part of the local evidence base. The Department of Energy and Climate Change (DECC) presented their new National Heat Map.
